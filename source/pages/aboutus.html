Our Farms

Colombo

We farm both vegetables and fruits in our Colombo farm.We raise a variety of vegetables including Beans,Tomatoes,Onions,Bellpepper.Also we harvest several kind of fruits such as Bananas,Papaya,Mangoes in Colombo farm.

Lindula

Lindula farm is the biggest farm that we have.Because of the mild climate,we harvest vegetables just like Broccoli,Raddish,Carrots,Cabbage and fruits just like Apples,Strawberries and Grapes.

QUALITY OF PRODUCTS

To ensure Customers re-order,quality standards need to be at the highest level.

At the point of Picking/Harvesting and Packaging,trained Quality Assurance staff are hands-on,as we are fully aware of the expectations of our consumers.Re-usable or Returnable styroform boxes with lids which keep produce chilled,fresh and safe from germs,till the point of delivery are transported in fully-enclosed trucks to ensure strict hygiene standards are achieved.

DELIVERY PROCESS

The practicality of delivering perishables daily,from almost 200KMS away to Colombo is daunting.To guarantee a smooth operation and minimize disappointing our valued customers,we will be carrying-out deliveries on Wednesday & Saturday.Deliveries will start as early as 7.30AM up to 7.30PM on these days.

Customers will be contacted by our delivery team,an hour in advance to coordinate collection.

Cut-off Time for Wednesday Deliveries: Tuesday 10.00AM

Cut-off Time for Saturday Deliveries: Friday 10.00AM

CONTACTLESS DELIVERY

As most orders will be paid for Online via Bank Transfer or Debit/Credit Card,the order can be left at a pre-designated point at the time of delivery.For cash-on-delivery transactions,the exact amount can be left in an envelope for our delivery team at pre-designated point.

Should there be any discrepancy with the order,after drop-off,the customer can contact the delivery team directly.
